Our Commitment to Your Data Privacy

To deliver these powerful features, FlexBookmark requires access to your bookmarks:

Why We Need Access

  • Read access: To organize and display your bookmarks effectively
  • Write access: To save and manage bookmarks as you direct
  • Sync capability: To keep your bookmarks updated across devices

Our Privacy Promise

  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • Zero bookmark data collected or stored on our servers
  • No tracking of your browsing history or personal information
  • Optional sync uses Google's secure infrastructure

Key Features

Smart Organization

Categorize bookmarks into easy-to-manage folders with intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Create hierarchical structures that match your workflow.

Instant Search

Find bookmarks in seconds with our smart search. Search by name, URL, tags or even page content for saved pages.

Cloud Sync

Sync bookmarks across all your devices. Access your bookmarks from computer, phone or tablet anytime.

Data Security

Your data is encrypted and protected. We respect your privacy and don't collect personal information.

Data Transparency

We only request bookmark access to:

  • Save and manage your bookmarks
  • Sync between your devices

Privacy Focused

All processing happens in your browser. We don't store your bookmarks on our servers. Sync uses Google's secure infrastructure.

How It Works

Install Extension

Visit Chrome Web Store and install FlexBookmark. The installation completes in just seconds.

Add Bookmarks

Click the FlexBookmark icon in your toolbar and select "Add current page" to save new bookmarks.

Organize

Arrange bookmarks into folders, add tags and descriptions for easy retrieval later.

Quick Access

Use keyboard shortcuts or click the icon to open bookmark manager anytime.
